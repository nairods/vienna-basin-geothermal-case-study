Why this study

The retreat investigates whether deep geothermal heat from the Vienna Basin can substantially decarbonise Vienna’s ~6 TWh/yr district-heating network — and what under-examined questions deserve attention given that Wien Energie + OMV are already building the first 20 MWth plant (Aspern, 2028).

Seven research threads, eight foundation blocks (F1–F8), nine reproducibility scripts, and 160 real earthquake events ground the analysis. Use the tabs to play with the headline calculations.
